Defending IaaS with ATT&CK
==========================
The defending infrastructure-as-a-service with ATT&CK research project provides analysts with a methodology to refine adversary behaviors and build unique threat collections based on observed cyber attacks that target their specific platform and attack surface.
This project is created and maintained by the `MITRE Engenuity Center for
Threat-Informed Defense <https://ctid.mitre-engenuity.org/>`__ in futherance of our
mission to advance the start of the art and and the state of the practice in
threat-informed defense globally. The project is funded by our `research participants
<https://ctid.mitre-engenuity.org/our-work/attack-flow/>`__.
